About BTC Signal

Affordable Bitcoin data & metrics for everyone

🎯

Our Mission

We believe quality Bitcoin data and metrics shouldn't cost $100+/month or require trusting anonymous "gurus" on social media.

BTC Signal was built to solve a simple problem: most retail traders either can't afford premium data tools like Glassnode or Coinglass, or they're stuck relying on influencers with hidden agendas. We wanted to create something different—transparent data and analysis available to anyone for the cost of a few satoshis.

✅ What We Are

  • A data service — We provide on-chain metrics, funding rates, liquidation data, and technical analysis
  • Data-driven — Our signals come from math and market data, not opinions
  • Affordable — Pay-per-use starting at 21 sats (~$0.02)
  • Transparent — We show our data sources and methodology openly
  • Privacy-focused — No accounts, no personal data, Lightning payments

❌ What We Are NOT

  • Not a trading platform — We don't execute trades or hold funds
  • Not financial advisors — We don't provide personalized advice
  • Not a "get rich quick" scheme — Trading is risky, we're just a data tool
  • Not a subscription trap — No recurring charges, pay only when you want
  • Not selling your data — We don't collect or sell personal information

Why We Built This

The crypto data space is broken. On one side, you have expensive institutional tools like Glassnode that cost hundreds per month. On the other, you have free "signals" from anonymous Twitter accounts who are often front-running their followers or getting paid to shill.

We saw an opportunity: aggregate useful Bitcoin data from public sources, and use the Lightning Network to make it accessible to everyone through micropayments. No subscriptions, no lock-in, no hidden fees.

Our data comes from transparent sources. We show you the math. No mysterious algorithms, no black boxes—just clear data and metrics you can verify.
