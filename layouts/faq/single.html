Data & Methodology

Where does your data come from?

We aggregate data from multiple reliable public sources:

  • Price Data: Binance US, CoinGecko, CoinCap (real-time spot prices)
  • Derivatives Data: Bybit, OKX (funding rates, open interest)
  • Market Sentiment: Alternative.me Fear & Greed Index
  • On-Chain Data: Mempool.space (hashrate, network metrics)
  • Market Cap: CoinGecko (dominance, global metrics)

All data is fetched from public APIs and cached periodically to ensure accuracy while avoiding rate limits.

How are signals generated?

Our signals are calculated using multiple data points:

  1. Technical Analysis: RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, moving averages, and chart patterns
  2. Derivatives Data: Funding rates, open interest, liquidation levels
  3. Market Sentiment: Fear & Greed Index, long/short ratios
  4. On-Chain Metrics: Hashrate trends, network activity

These factors are combined to generate a directional bias (bullish/bearish/neutral) with a confidence score. No black boxes—you can verify our data sources.

How often is analysis updated?

We publish new analysis posts regularly throughout the day. Dashboard widgets update in real-time (every 30 seconds to 5 minutes depending on the metric). Market conditions are monitored 24/7.

What timeframe do your signals cover?

Our primary analysis focuses on the 4-hour (4H) timeframe, which balances short-term trading opportunities with noise reduction. However, we also consider daily and weekly trends for context. Each analysis post specifies the relevant timeframe.

How accurate are your signals?

We track and publish our historical performance transparently on the Dashboard. Like any analysis method, our signals are not 100% accurate. Past performance does not guarantee future results. We provide confidence scores with each signal to indicate the strength of the analysis.

Technical & Support

The data isn't loading. What should I do?

Try these steps:

  1. Refresh the page
  2. Check your internet connection
  3. Disable any ad blockers (they may block API calls)
  4. Try a different browser
  5. Clear your browser cache

Some data sources may occasionally be unavailable due to API rate limits or maintenance.

My payment went through but content is still locked?

Lightning payments are instant, but occasionally there may be a brief delay. Try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, your browser may have blocked the local storage where access is tracked. Try disabling strict privacy settings or using a different browser.
