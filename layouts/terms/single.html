Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ now.Format "January 2, 2006" }}

The Short Version

We collect almost nothing. No accounts, no emails required, no tracking pixels, no selling data. We built BTCSignals Pro to be as privacy-respecting as possible.

1. Information We Don't Collect

Unlike most websites, here's what we don't do:

  • We don't require account creation
  • We don't ask for your name, email, or personal details to access content
  • We don't use tracking pixels or advertising cookies
  • We don't sell or share data with third parties
  • We don't store your Lightning wallet addresses
  • We don't track which specific content you view

2. Information That May Be Collected

Browser Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage to remember:

  • Which posts you've unlocked (so you don't have to pay again)
  • Your access pass expiration (hourly, daily, weekly)
  • Your theme preference (dark/light mode)

This data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers. Clearing your browser data will reset this information.

Newsletter Subscription (Optional)

If you voluntarily subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address. This is:

  • Stored securely with our email service provider
  • Used only to send you market updates
  • Never sold or shared with third parties
  • Easy to unsubscribe at any time

Server Logs

Our hosting provider (Netlify) may collect standard server logs including:

  • IP addresses
  • Browser type
  • Pages visited
  • Timestamps

These logs are used for security and performance monitoring only and are automatically deleted after a short retention period.

Lightning Payments

When you make a Lightning payment:

  • We receive a payment confirmation from our payment processor
  • We do not see your wallet address or identity
  • Payment records are minimal and used only to verify access

3. Cookies

We use minimal, functional cookies only:

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic site functionality
  • No advertising cookies: We don't show ads
  • No third-party tracking: No Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, etc.

4. Third-Party Services

Our site interacts with the following external services:

  • CoinGecko API: For real-time price data (no user data sent)
  • CoinGecko API: For market data (no user data sent)
  • Alternative.me API: For Fear & Greed Index (no user data sent)
  • Netlify: Our hosting provider (their privacy policy)

5. Data Security

We protect your information by:

  • Using HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Not storing sensitive personal data in the first place
  • Using Lightning Network for payments (inherently private)
  • Keeping our codebase simple and auditable

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your local storage at any time (removes all local data)
  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter if subscribed
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns
  • Request information about what data we have (spoiler: probably none)

7. Children's Privacy

BTCSignals Pro is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact Us

Questions about this privacy policy? Reach out:

  • Twitter: @BTCSignal_ai
